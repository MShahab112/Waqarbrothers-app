☁️ Cloud Backup (Google Drive / OneDrive)

When a folder is set here, every manual backup and every Day Close will also be copied there in addition to the normal local download. The app never talks to Google or Microsoft — it just writes files to the folder, and your sync client (OneDrive / Google Drive for Desktop) uploads them in the background.

Just installed on a new machine? Click Auto-detect below — if OneDrive or Google Drive is already signed in, the app will find the sync folder and configure itself.

Leave empty to disable cloud backup. Local downloads always work.

🖼️ Receipt Images

From v1.8 onwards, every new receipt you attach is also copied to <cloud>/uploads/ automatically. Use the buttons below for one-time sync of older receipts or to recover images on a new computer after a database restore.

Backup Receipts Now → uploads all local images to cloud (run this once on your old computer).
Restore Receipts from Cloud → downloads all cloud images to this computer (run this on your new computer after restoring the database).
